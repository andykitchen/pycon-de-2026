Why systematize?

Systems crystallize judgement

...by building coherent derived judgements
from base judgements

What is a judgement?

A working, stylized fact about the world.

If you can make complex compound judgements directly, then you don't need a system.

Systems are useful when:
  • The system can be practically applied
  • The base judgements are easy, clear and less controversial
